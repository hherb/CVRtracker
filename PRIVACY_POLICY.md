# Privacy Policy for CVRtracker

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## Overview

CVRtracker ("the App") is a cardiovascular health tracking application developed by Horst Herb. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our App.

**We value your privacy. CVRtracker is designed with a privacy-first approachâ€”your health data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.**

## Information We Collect

### Health Data You Provide

The App allows you to enter and track:

- **Blood Pressure Readings**: Systolic and diastolic values with timestamps
- **Lipid Panel Data**: Total cholesterol, HDL, LDL, and triglyceride values
- **Profile Information**: Age, sex, smoking status, diabetes status, and treatment information used for cardiovascular risk calculations

### Apple HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, the App can:

- Read blood pressure and heart rate data from Apple Health
- Write blood pressure readings to Apple Health

**HealthKit data is only accessed with your authorization and is handled according to Apple's strict HealthKit guidelines.**

## How Your Data Is Stored

### Local Storage Only

All data entered into CVRtracker is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your health information:

- Is **never uploaded** to external servers
- Is **never shared** with third parties
- Is **never used** for advertising or analytics
- Remains under **your complete control**

### iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled for the App, your data may be synced across your Apple devices through your personal iCloud account. This sync is:

- Encrypted end-to-end by Apple
- Controlled by your personal Apple ID settings
- Not accessible to the App developer

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.**

The App does not:

- Collect analytics or usage data
- Use third-party tracking services
- Send data to external servers
- Share information with advertisers
- Sell or monetize your health data in any way

## Apple HealthKit Integration

When you choose to enable HealthKit integration:

- The App will request specific permissions for blood pressure and heart rate data
- You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings
- HealthKit data is never used for advertising or shared with third parties
- All HealthKit usage complies with Apple's HealthKit guidelines

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:

- iOS device encryption
- App sandboxing (the App cannot access data from other apps)
- Apple's Secure Enclave for device-level security
- Optional device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID protection

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: View all your data within the App at any time
- **Delete**: Remove individual readings or all data through the App
- **Export**: Your data remains accessible on your device
- **HealthKit Permissions**: Manage in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health

## Children's Privacy

CVRtracker is not intended for use by children under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Medical Disclaimer

CVRtracker is for informational purposes only and is not intended to replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider regarding any medical conditions or health concerns.
